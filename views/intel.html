Intelligence and Surveillance

AI techniques are being implemented to increase the accuracy of intelligence and surveillance. Machine learning and computer vision software can be used for surveillance operations. This software has the potential to sort through large sums of data for insights faster than trained human analysts.

The Central Intelligence Agency is incorporating computer “vision” and machine learning algorithms to sort through tons of footage to identify hostile activity for targeting. Other programs, such as TRACE, are beginning to us AI techniques to automatically locate and identify targets with the help of Synthetic Radar images.
